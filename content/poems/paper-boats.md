We folded worlds from yesterday's news,
and set them sailing down the street.
Small captains of the puddled world,
with rain for wind and thunder for a drum.

They never made it very far —
a gutter, a stone, a sudden turn.
But oh, the oceans they crossed
in the ten small minutes they lived.
